Questions?

What phone do I need?

Any Android phone running Android 8.0+ (Oreo or newer). iPhone is not supported.

Will it text every single missed call?

You control it. Set quiet hours (no texts at 2am), block specific numbers, and set a cooldown so repeat callers don't get spammed.

Is this legal?

They called you first ‚Äî that's consent to respond. Include opt-out language ("Reply STOP") and only auto-reply to inbound callers. This is not cold texting.

Do you see my texts or calls?

No. Everything runs locally on your phone. We never see your messages, call log, or any personal data.

Why sideload instead of Play Store?

Google restricts SMS/Call Log permissions for Play Store apps. The sideload version has full auto-send capability. A Play Store version (tap-to-send) is coming soon.
