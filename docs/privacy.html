Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 17, 2026

AutoText Hub ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data the app accesses and how it's used.

What Data Does AutoText Hub Access?

AutoText Hub requires the following permissions to function:

  • Notification Access: To detect missed call notifications from your phone's dialer app.
  • Call Log (sideload version only): To retrieve the phone number of the most recent missed call.
  • Send SMS (sideload version only): To automatically send your pre-configured reply message.

What Data Do We Collect?

None.

AutoText Hub operates entirely on your device. We do not:

  • Collect or transmit your call history
  • Store your text messages
  • Access your contacts
  • Send any data to external servers
  • Use analytics or tracking

Local Data Storage

The app stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Your message templates
  • Blocked number list
  • Settings (quiet hours, cooldown period, etc.)
  • Message log (sent messages with timestamps)

This data never leaves your phone and is deleted if you uninstall the app.

Third-Party Services

AutoText Hub does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks.

TCPA Compliance

AutoText Hub is designed for transactional responses to inbound calls only. We recommend including opt-out language ("Reply STOP to opt out") in your message templates to maintain compliance with telecommunications regulations.

Children's Privacy

AutoText Hub is not intended for use by children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
